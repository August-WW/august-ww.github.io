History of August

In 2020, the world had shut down due to the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic.
During this time, my family and I never went anywhere. Everything was closed and we wanted to be safe.

During this time, I got really bored. Socializing was really hard. I decided to make a Twitter account.

It was pretty cool at the time. A few weeks after signing up, I saw a hashtag trending for Fursuit Friday.
I was confused, but I expected it to be pictures of furries right away. I was right.

After scrolling and scrolling, I started to think about how cool it all was! I started to look more into what furries were.

Eventually, I went online to find a reference sheet base to color to make a fursona of my own!
Blue has always been my favorite color, so I opened Photoshop and started coloring.

That's how August was born
